Welcome to Tsuki Ramen!

Our Story

Founded in 2022, Tsuki Ramen brings the authentic mouthwatering tastes of the Pacific to the beautiful city of London, Ontario! Serving dishes such as Japanese ramen bowls as big as the moon, delicious Hawaiian poke bowls made with the freshest ingredients, as well as a wide variety of savoury Asian appetizers, you can be sure to enjoy a taste of the islands. Whether it be spicy, creamy, seafood, or vegetarian you're in the mood for, we've got options for everyone. We also make sure to source our ingredients locally to guarantee quality and support our fellow businesses, come try us out today!
